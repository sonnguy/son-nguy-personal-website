# docs/MASTER_BUILD_BRIEF.md

## Project Overview

Build a personal brand website for Son Nguy.

This website is not a developer portfolio, project showcase, or blog.

It is a recruiter-facing professional landing page designed to position Son Nguy as a Product Engineer with deep expertise in Payments, Martech, Customer Experience Platforms, and AI-assisted engineering workflows.

## Primary Goal

Help visitors quickly understand:

* Who Son Nguy is
* What problems he solves
* Why he is different from typical frontend engineers
* What platforms and business domains he has worked on
* How to contact him

## Target Audience

Priority order:

1. Engineering Managers
2. CTOs
3. Startup Founders
4. Recruiters

## Positioning Statement

Product Engineer specializing in Payments, Martech, and Customer Experience Platforms.

## Key Differentiators

Do not position Son primarily as:

* React Developer
* Frontend Specialist
* JavaScript Engineer

Instead emphasize:

* Product Engineering
* Payment Systems
* Martech & Analytics
* Customer Experience Platforms
* AI-Assisted Development

## Brand Personality

* Professional
* Modern
* Trustworthy
* Product-focused
* Senior
* Minimal
* Content-first

## Visual Direction

Inspired by:

* Stripe

* Whitespace is valuable

* Avoid developer-template aesthetics
